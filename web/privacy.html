Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 25, 2026

Thank you for using Yoke. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the Yoke mobile application ("App").

1. Information We Collect

Information you provide:

  • Name and email address when you create an account
  • Devotional posts and reflections you write
  • Comments you leave on other users' posts
  • Group names and membership
  • Notification preferences and app settings

Information collected automatically:

  • Device type and operating system
  • App usage data (which screens you visit, features you use)
  • Push notification tokens for sending daily reminders

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage your Yoke account
  • Display your devotional posts to your friends and groups based on your privacy settings
  • Send you daily Bible passage notifications at your chosen time
  • Send account confirmation and password reset emails
  • Improve the App and fix bugs
  • Respond to your support requests

3. How We Share Your Information

With other users:

  • Posts you mark as Public are visible to all Yoke users
  • Posts you mark as Friends Only are visible only to your accepted friends
  • Posts you mark as Group are visible only to members of that group
  • Your name, Yoke code, and profile information are visible to other users

With third-party services:

We use the following services to operate the App:

  • Supabase — database and authentication (supabase.com)
  • Expo / Apple / Google — push notification delivery
  • RevenueCat — subscription and payment management
  • Resend — transactional email delivery

We do not sell your personal information to anyone.

4. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your personal information and posts will be permanently deleted within 30 days.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Opt out of marketing communications

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at contact@yokefaith.com.

6. Children's Privacy

Yoke is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately at contact@yokefaith.com.

7. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information using industry-standard encryption and secure infrastructure. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by sending an email to your registered address or displaying a notice in the App. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

  • Email: contact@yokefaith.com
  • Website: yokefaith.com
